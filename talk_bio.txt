Bio:

Jack Hessel is a reseacher at Samaya AI, a knowledge discovery startup. Previously, he was a postdoc+research scientist at the Allen Institute for AI, and before that, he earned a PhD from Cornell. His work, which spans language processing, machine learning, and computer vision, has been reconized with several awards, including ACL 2023 best paper and EMNLP 2023 outstanding paper.
