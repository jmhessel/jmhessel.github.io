caption explanationIn the business world, a hostile takeover is a situation in which one company attempts to take control of another one by appealing directly to stockholders, rather than getting cooperation from the board of directors and CEO. While a hostile takeover involves some amount of pressure, it is nothing like an actual battle in which hostilities take place. The joke here is that the sword stuck in the desktop represents that a battle was fought in this office, and the hostile takeover actually involved fighting and weapons, as opposed to merely strongarm corporate business tactics.
