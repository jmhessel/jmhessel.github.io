caption explanationThere are a couple of different plays on words involved to the joke. The suspects in the police line-up are what is known as a matryoshka doll. When you open the largest doll you'll find a smaller one and inside each one is another smaller doll. The cop is saying he got the doll to "open up" meaning to confess or literally open, and now the doll is beside herself. Beside herself can mean a person is angry but in this case it means literally beside herself.
