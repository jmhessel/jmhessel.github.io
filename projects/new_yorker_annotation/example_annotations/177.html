caption explanationThis caption is relying upon the concept of "old money" in which people who are wealthy sometimes come from families that made fortunes many generations prior and the children, grandchildren, etc are pretty much financially set for life because of the success of their past family members. As a result, old money families/individuals are often seen as having more refined tastes and more traditional signs of wealth, such as large, lavish homes, as compared to wealthy individuals who've come to have their wealth only more recently, as a result of stocks or cryptocurrency. In this comic, the caveman in the ditch is look at the caveman in the large cave and his remark is implying that the caveman in the large cave came from "old money". However, the joke here is that these people are in prehistoric times, a time before things that most people consider to be "old" so rather than saying the caveman in the cave "comes from old money" he says he "comes from prehistoric money".
