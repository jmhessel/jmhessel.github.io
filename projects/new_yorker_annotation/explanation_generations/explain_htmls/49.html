Mrs. Freeley buried his Scotch again.

  • The joke is funny because the man in the sandbox is clearly drunk, and the kids are staring at him. The man is saying that the reason he's in the sandbox is because his wife hid his bottle of Scotch whiskey and he's trying to find it. This joke plays on the stereotype that drunk people often act like children.

  • The joke is funny because the man in the sandbox is likely there because he is looking for his hidden bottle of Scotch whisky, which his wife has probably hidden from him again. The joke implies that the man has a drinking problem, and is willing to search through a sandbox (a place associated with childhood innocence) to find his alcohol.

  • The joke is funny because it's a play on words. The man in the sandbox is looking for his Scotch (whisky) but can't find it. The joke is that Mrs. Freeley (a homophone of "freely") has hidden the whisky somewhere on the property, and the man is looking for it in the sandbox. This is a clever way to hide alcohol from adults, as they would typically not think to look in a sandbox for their drinks.
