Is the salmon fresh?

  • The joke is funny because the man is more concerned with whether or not the salmon is fresh, rather than the fact that there is a bear in the restaurant. This is a reversal of the expected reaction, as most people would be more concerned with the bear than the food.

  • The joke is funny because the man is nonchalantly asking about the freshness of the salmon dish on the menu, as if it's not unusual for there to be a wild bear in the restaurant. This is a play on the fact that salmon is often served fresh, as in, recently caught. In this case, the salmon would be very fresh because it was just brought in by the bear.

  • The joke is funny because the man is more concerned with the quality of the food than the fact that there is a bear in the restaurant. The bear is out of place and is a potentially dangerous animal, but the man is only worried about whether or not the salmon is fresh. This shows how ridiculous it is to worry about such a trivial thing when there is a much bigger issue at hand.
