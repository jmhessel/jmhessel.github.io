Remember that domino factory?

  • The joke is funny because it's a reference to a scene in the movie "The Big Lebowski" in which a domino factory is demolished by a wrecking ball. The joke implies that the current scene is similar to that movie scene, but with bowling pins instead of dominoes.

  • The joke is funny because the men are talking about how they demolished a domino factory with a wrecking ball, but the place they are currently demolishing looks like it should be demolished with bowling balls. The joke is a play on words, as the two objects are similar sounding but have different uses.

  • The joke is funny because it's a reference to the game of dominoes, which is similar to bowling. In dominoes, players knock down dominoes that are arranged in a line; in bowling, players knock down pins that are arranged in a triangle. The joke is that the men are preparing to demolish a bowling pin alley with a wrecking ball, which is not how either game is typically played.
