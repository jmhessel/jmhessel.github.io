If we hadn't eaten Ted, he really would have enjoyed this.

  • The joke is funny because it is a play on words. The word "dolphin" can mean both the sea mammal, and a type of fish. So, the joke is making a pun on the fact that the two men on the island have eaten Ted, who is also a dolphin.

  • The joke is funny because it is a play on the common phrase "if only." In this case, the speaker is saying that if they had not eaten their friend ( Ted), he would be able to enjoy the current situation. The fact that the situation is dolphins forming an island makes it even funnier.

  • The joke is funny because of the dark humor of cannibalism. The men are stranded on the island and have resorted to cannibalism to survive. One of the men jokes that if they hadn't eaten their friend Ted, he would have enjoyed the view of the dolphins forming an island.
