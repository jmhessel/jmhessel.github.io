Are you willing to relocate?

  • The joke is funny because the man's boss is so far away that he might as well be on another planet. The joke is also funny because it plays on the common frustration of having to deal with a lazy or incompetent boss.

  • The joke is funny because the boss is so far away from the man, relocating would require the man to move closer to the boss, which is the opposite of what the man wants.

  • The joke is funny because it's a common thing for people to be asked to move to a different location for their job. However, in this case, the person being asked to move is not being asked to move to a different city or country, but to a different chair. The fact that the man is willing to move six chairs closer to his boss in order to keep his job shows how absurd the request is.
