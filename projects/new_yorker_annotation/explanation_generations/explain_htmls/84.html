Remember when your rubber seal broke? I'm pregnant.

  • The joke is funny because it's a play on words. The woman is saying that she is pregnant, but the man's head being a blender makes it sound like she is saying that the man's rubber seal (on the blender) broke, and thus he is leaking. The double-meaning is what makes this joke funny.

  • The joke is funny because the woman is telling the man that she is pregnant, which is a big deal, and he is nonchalantly responding with something that sounds like an unrelated memory about a rubber seal. This is funny because it's a non-sequitur, and also because it's a reminder that, even though pregnancy is a serious matter, it can be caused by something as mundane as a broken rubber seal.

  • The joke is funny because the woman is telling the man (whose head has been replaced with a blender) that she is pregnant, and the man can't hear her because he is a blender. The joke is also a play on the fact that blenders often have a rubber seal that can break, and when that happens, it is very easy for things to get stuck in the blades, which can lead to pregnancy.
