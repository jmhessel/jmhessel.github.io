Say something viral.

  • The joke is funny because the man with the camera is recording the party, and he wants the woman to say something that will get a lot of views on the internet (go "viral"). But the woman is perplexed, because she doesn't understand what he's talking about. The joke is clever because it playfully pokes at the way that people often try to get others to do things that they don't understand, in the hopes that it will benefit them.

  • The joke is funny because the man with the camera on his head is hoping to capture something "viral" on video, i.e., something that will be widely shared online. But given the context --- a dinner party --- it's unlikely that anything interesting will happen, let alone something that would be worthy of going viral.
