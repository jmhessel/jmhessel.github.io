The restroom is just down the hall and anywhere you want.

  • The joke is funny because it's a play on the common trope of a woman being in a man's restroom. In this case, the woman is in a caveman's dwelling, which is effectively a cave. The caveman tells her that she can use the restroom anywhere she wants, which is a humorous way of saying that there are no restrooms in a cave.

  • The joke is funny because it is a play on the fact that the woman is sitting in a cave. The caveman is telling her that the restroom is down the hall, but since there are no hallways or bathrooms in a cave, the joke is that she can go to the restroom anywhere she wants. This is a play on the fact that people usually have to go to the restroom in a specific place.

  • The joke is funny because it's a play on the common trope that cavemen are dirty and live in caves because they haven't discovered hygiene yet. In this case, the woman is telling the caveman that he can use the restroom anywhere he wants, implying that there are no restrooms in the cave. This would be dirty and unhygienic, which is why it's funny.
