We'll really miss you.

  • The joke is funny because the corporate boardroom is a place where people are usually very serious and focused on work. The fact that there are people dancing on the table in this context is very unusual and not professional. The joke is further funny because the man who is angry at the dancers is saying that they will be missed, which is typically something you say when someone leaves a job voluntarily, not when they are being fired.

  • The joke is funny because the dancing office workers are being unprofessional and are being asked to leave. The man who is angry at them says that they will miss them, but he is being sarcastic. The joke is clever because it is a play on words.

  • The joke is funny because the man's statement is a typical thing to say to someone who is leaving a job, but in this case, the person is not leaving voluntarily and is instead being fired. The man's statement is thus an ironic comment on the situation.
