Let's just shoot the next one.

  • The joke is funny because it's a non-sequitur. The pirates are discussing what to do with the man who just jumped off the plank, and one suggests shooting the next person, as if that would be a more sensible solution. Of course, shooting someone is a much more violent solution than simply making them walk the plank, so the joke is humorous because it's a disproportionate response.

  • The joke is funny because it's a non-violent solution to the problem of what to do with someone who wants to walk the plank. Pirates are commonly known for being violent, so the fact that they would rather just shoot the person than make them walk the plank is unexpected.

  • The joke is funny because it's a non-sequitur. The pirates are shocked that the man would rather dive into the sea than walk the plank, and their reaction is to shoot the next person instead. This is absurd because it's much easier to make someone walk the plank than it is to shoot them.
