Before we start, would anyone else like some water?

  • The joke is funny because it's a typical thing to say at the start of a meeting, but in this case, it's ironic because the fern cannot drink water. The fern is also not likely to start the meeting since it cannot talk.

  • The joke is funny because someone has made the fern the boss of the company, which is absurd. The person who made the fern the boss is now trying to be helpful by offering water to everyone in the meeting, as one would normally do for their boss, but it's funny because it's a fern.

  • The joke is funny because the person who asked the question doesn't seem to realize that the fern cannot drink water. They are treating the fern as if it is a person, which is absurd.
