I don't want you to be the one that got away.

  • The joke is funny because it is a play on words. The phrase "the one that got away" is often used to describe a person who you let slip through your fingers, usually referring to a romantic partner. In this case, the man is literally "the one that got away" because he is bound to his chair and cannot leave.

  • The joke is funny because the man is clearly restraining the woman against her will, which is not usually considered romantic. The fact that he doesn't want her to be the one that got away implies that he is planning to kill her, which is even more sinister.
