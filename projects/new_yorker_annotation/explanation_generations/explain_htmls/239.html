He won't last long.

  • The joke is funny because it is a play on the fact that clones are often not as stable as the originals. In this case, the clone is made of bubbles, which are not very stable, and thus the joke is that the clone won't last long.

  • The joke is funny because of the nonchalant way the men discuss the man turning into a bubble. This suggests that, to them, it is a regular occurrence and not something to be alarmed about. The fact that the man is turning into a bubble is unusual, and the joke pokes fun at the way people can become complacent with even the most bizarre things.

  • The joke is funny because the two men are talking about the bubble man as if he is a clone who is going to "expire" or die soon. This is a play on the fact that clones are often seen as expendable and not as real people.
