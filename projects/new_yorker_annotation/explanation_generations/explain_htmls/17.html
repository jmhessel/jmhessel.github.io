Take it. You mother worries.

  • The joke is funny because it's a play on words. The phrase "take it" could mean either to take the sweater or to accept God's gift of the ten commandments. But in this case, the phrase is being used to tell Moses to take the sweater because his mother is worried that he'll catch a cold. The joke is clever because it's a double meaning that is also a pun.
