Head for the carousel! It's our only chance!

  • The joke is funny because it's a play on the common trope in westerns of the heroes escaping on a horse-drawn carriage/carousel. In this case, the heroes are trying to escape on a horse-drawn carousel, but since they're on toy rocking horses, it's not going to be very effective. The fact that they're still trying anyway, despite the clear disadvantage, is what makes it funny.

  • The joke is funny because it's a play on a common western trope, where the heroes are being chased by the bad guys and they head for the safety of the nearest town. In this case, the nearest town is a carousel, which is a child's toy and not a actual town. The contrast between the seriousness of the situation and the silliness of the solution is what makes the joke funny.

  • The joke is funny because it's a play on the common movie/tv trope of the protagonists being chased by the bad guys, and their only chance of escape is to get to a specific location. In this case, the location is a carousel, which is not only an unusual choice, but is also impossible to escape on because the horses don't move. This highlights the absurdity of the situation, and the fact that the men are still taking it seriously despite being on toy horses makes it even funnier.
